Appearance


When an outsider sees him, they might think: "no one special, just another person passing by".

And oh my, how terribly wrong would they be.

A long, brown-ish and grey cloak, its hood casting shadow on the face beneath.

If someone looks, they will see: a man, with skin two or three shades darken than you might expect in this part of the world. Short black beard and hair. But anyone who saw that was fooled.

The illusion hides a face of terror, rotting flesh and skin, with bones flashing from underneath, entirely red eyes. And despite this appearance not it is the part that may scare the most, this being the fact that all this functions normally, like the creature was still living.

Under the cloak - a purple and dark blue robe with a few yellow accents, reinforced with an addition of padded leather patches in important places, in order to reduce mostly the blunt damage.


Personality

Although a lich, which is an undead being, stripped of any feelings, Avari retains some of his traits from the "previous life". Of course his goal is ultimately to serve the Supreme Beings with all feats he has, but he behaves just like a human. He sees others not by their might but what of a person they are (of course excluding the Allmighty 41), but is intelligent enough to know when he should keep his tongue behind his teeth.

This very thing regarding his character has been born long ago from Shijuuten's urge to redefine things. Thus, an undead who genuinly enjoys talking to others, thinks on his own, and his emotions aren't supressed as hard as those of Momonga has been created.

Bio


Once, there was a wizard. He was helping people, taking missions and dealing with monsters when he had to.

But as time passed, he was shifting more and more to other things. He studied magic. Old tomes, covered in dust he searched through. In order to finally become stronger.

As much as he wanted to, he could not reach his dream: to finally advance to being a 3rd tier user.

He cut himself off from people so as to have more time to learn, study and practice. But then, he knew he was running out of it. His life wasn't to last for much longer.

Therefore, he resorted to using the ultimate means; he broke the seals on one of the books he had and began to study. The dark magic, which in no long corrupted him. His heart.

But it didn't turn him into a being of pure evil, seeking prey in constant bloodlust. No, it was something worse, as he still apeared as if nothing changed.

Ultimately he has been killed by a team of golden-ranked adventurers, those being equal in strength to him.

His spirit, however, could leave. Bound to the body it survived - and in the end, he got a new one. To serve the Supreme Ones.
